Practice As A Rhythm

Community Questionnaire

We are always practicing something. We can center our values when we make our habits or practices conscious — right down to our body posture. Combining the critical inquiry of Erica Hunt, Alexis Pauline Gumbs, and Richard Strozzi-Heckler, this form is intended to serve as a tuning fork to help us embody what feels most resonant and to dream beyond paradigms of domination and extraction.
